"SOC Role in Blue Team:"
    * Blue Team is about defensive security
    [Roles]
        - L1 Analysts: Junior members who triage alerts and pass complex cases to L2
        - L2 Analysts: Experienced members who investigate more advanced attacks
        - Engineers: Experts in configuring security tools like EDR or SIEM
        - Manager: A person who manages the whole SOC team

    * If SOC finds a BIG problem, and doesn't have the expertise to solve:

    "Cyber Incident Response Team (CIRT)"
        * Members have a broad knowledge of threats and do not need to rely on tools like SIEM.
        * Includes malware analysts, threat hunters, forensics experts, threat intel experts etc.
        * [MSSP]    a cyber security company providing SOC services
